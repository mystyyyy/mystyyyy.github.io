About

Hello my name is Brandon H Nguyen, and I go by Mysty online! I’m a concept artist and illustrationist.

I doodled all the time ever since I was a little kid, and growing up on the internet, I was also exposed to various flash games, anime, and World of Warcraft. The characters and worlds I was put in enamored me to no end. I love imagining my own scenes with fantastical characters inspired by others, and more importantly, put it down on the paper!

The internet also exposed me to many artists, one importantly being Ross Tran, or Ross Draws. He made me realize my love for art was more than just a hobby. With my realization of passion, I engrossed myself with all the resources related to concept art and illustration I could find so I could make better art and can fully realize my ideas.

Art is like breathing to me. It’s the natural thing to do, and I can only stop when I die!
