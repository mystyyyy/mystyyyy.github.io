Hello my name is Brandon H Nguyen, and I go by Mysty online! I’m a concept and illustration artist.

I was born in the Lehigh Valley in Pennsylvania, and have always gravitated towards art. I always had the urge to create things, and with access to only a pencil and paper, art was an easy response to satiate the urge. I love doodling on my classwork and homework (my teachers were usually ok with this!), imagining scenes of badass warriors, mages, and rangers fighting each other with otherworldly powers.

Growing up on the internet I played tons of flash games, was exposed to anime, and thought that World of Warcraft was the coolest thing ever. I was also exposed to video content from artists like Draw with Jazza, and importantly, Ross Tran. He made me realize that my love for art was more than just a fun little hobby. And thus, I was thrown into the concept art and illustration rabbit hole.

I was initially enrolled in a performing arts highschool as a vocalist (singing is fun!), but switched to visual art after freshman year to truly engross myself in art. I dug up all the resources I could find online so I can improve as fast as I can.

I have always created art, and will do so until the day I die. It’s like breathing to me, and you can’t make me stop!
